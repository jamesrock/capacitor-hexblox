Click/press to rotate, use arrow keys or swipe to move.

The more lines cleared in a single move, the more points are scored. Reach a new level for every 10 lines cleared. The higer the level, the quicker bricks fall, the more points are scored.

Toggle on Free-Flow mode (FF) to prevent bricks from auto-falling - this may also be used to pause the game.

Fully inspired by the GameBoy variant of TETRIS, using the original Nintendo Rotation System (NRS) and scoring system.

Enjoy!
